HTHSRobotics & Coding Club

The Robotics & Coding Club is one of HTHS' largest clubs. This club serves to connect students interested in the field of robotics with those interested in software development. Students teach each other various skills and concepts — from breadboarding to soldering, from writing pragmatic code to utilizing defensive security techniques — as well as participate in competitions such as the American Computer Science League (ACSL), the VEX Robotics Competition (VRC), and assorted Capture the Flag (CTF) contests.
